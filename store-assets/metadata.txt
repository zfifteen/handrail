Description:
Handrail is a local-first iPhone companion for Codex Desktop on your Mac. Pair your iPhone with the Handrail desktop CLI on the same local network, then view Codex chats, follow task progress, continue existing chats, and stop running work from your phone.

Handrail does not run a cloud workspace, does not require an account, and does not upload your chat data to a Handrail server. Codex Desktop remains local and authoritative on your Mac. The iPhone app connects to the Handrail CLI over your local Wi-Fi or LAN using a per-device pairing token.

Handrail works with OpenAI Codex Desktop. Handrail is not affiliated with OpenAI.

Review notes:
Handrail requires Local Network access to connect to the Handrail CLI running on the user's Mac. The connection uses ws:// on the local network and is protected by a per-device pairing token. No Handrail backend account, sync service, payment, telemetry, or cloud relay is used.
